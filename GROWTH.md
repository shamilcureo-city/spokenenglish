# Speakwell — the realistic path to millions in India

> Grounded in research on how comparable apps actually scaled (and died): **Hello English**
> (50M users on ~$6.6M raised, mostly organic ASO), **Stimuler** (near-identical voice-first
> AI English app, IIT-BHU, $3.75M — **4M+ downloads but only ~45k payers ≈ 1.1% conversion**,
> India only its #3 market), **Enguru** (B2B2C with NSDC/Tata/Magic Bus), **Duolingo** (free +
> habit-loop + memetic social), **ShareChat** (1M→10M via WhatsApp forwarding), and **Byju's**
> (the CAC grave: ~80% of revenue, $4.6B+ burned, collapse).

**One brutal truth up front:** "millions of users" and "a real business" are two *different,
partly opposing* problems. This plan treats **millions of FREE users** as the job of the growth
engine, and **a thin paid slice + B2B2C** as the job of the revenue engine — and **never funds
growth with paid ads.**

---

## 1. The wedge & positioning

**Who first (and ONLY first):** the **Tier-2/3 job-seeker / fresher who can read & write English
but FREEZES when speaking.** Start with **one language — Hindi** — then Telugu/Tamil. Do not
spread across five languages on day one.

Why this user: the pain is acute and *dated* (interviews, BPO voice rounds, placement cells).
Tier-2/3 hiring grew 21–23% YoY in 2025; only 32% of Indian jobseekers feel interview-confident,
and 44% of English-medium vs 26% of regional-language candidates feel confident. Word-of-mouth in
India fires on **jobs + status, not "fun."** "I can speak now" is inherently broadcast-worthy.

**The one-line promise (lead with emotion, never grammar):**
> **"Stop freezing. Speak English without fear — and we'll explain every mistake in YOUR language."**

**The moat** isn't "learn English." It's the intersection no incumbent owns: human-like
**unscripted AI conversation** + **mother-tongue feedback during live speech** + **5-min/day
habit** + **affordable**. Translating the UI is table stakes; *explaining your mistake in Tamil,
mid-conversation* is the moat. AI = **zero marginal human cost = the only structural way to reach
millions** where human-tutor apps (Clapingo ₹14 Cr, EngVarta 2M learners) physically cannot.

**The "thank-you" moment (design backward from this):** a user finishes their **first unscripted
5-minute conversation**, is told *in Hindi* "you froze on past tense — here's the one fix," tries
again, and **doesn't freeze.** Onboarding exists only to reach that moment in **<90 seconds, no
signup.**

## 2. The ONE growth loop

There is exactly **one** self-reinforcing engine; everything else is a spark or amplifier feeding it.

```
   ACQUIRE                ACTIVATE              WIN MOMENT
   tap a WhatsApp/   →    first 5-min      →    streak hit / "before→after"
   creator link;         unscripted talk,       clip / level-up / certificate
   PWA opens 1-tap       NO signup, fix          ("I can speak English now")
        ▲                in Hindi                       │
        │                                               ▼
        │                                       SHARE (Day 3+, after a win)
        └───────────────────────────────────   one-tap WhatsApp: result card /
            (deep link back into the PWA)        30-sec clip + "practise WITH me"
                                                 invite, pre-filled in mother tongue
```

**Why status, not cash:** a budget-less founder can't run cash referrals (device-farm fraud). The
loop uses **status + access**:
1. **The shareable artifact IS the marketing.** Auto-generate a **result card** (fluency score,
   streak, "MTI words fixed") and a **30-sec before→after speaking clip**. English fluency is a
   job/status signal, so users *want* to post it to **WhatsApp Status** (their whole contact list,
   free) and LinkedIn — each a free ad with a deep link. (Duolingo's milestone shares drove +190%
   YoY organic social impressions.)
2. **Turn the core pain into the invite:** the user's hardest problem is *"I have no one to
   practise with."* So the invite is **"Invite a friend so you BOTH get a practice partner"** (+
   both unlock Pro days / the Advanced module — **zero marginal cost**). A real per-cluster network
   effect, fired at peak motivation.

**Share UX = exactly two buttons:** "Share on WhatsApp" (pre-filled vernacular + deep link) and
"Copy link." Ask on **Day 3 after a win**, never Day 1. Email is dead in India.

**Honest math:** sustained K>1 is a fantasy here. Realistic **K = 0.5–0.7** → the loop supplies
~25–40% of signups and cuts blended CAC 30–40%, amplifying the creator spark ~1.5–2×. That's the
ceiling — still enormous when the spark is free.

## 3. Channel sequencing — the real engine

Three channels, strict priority. (The PWA's one-tap-open is a cold-start advantage on cheap
phones, but it **locks the Play-Store ASO engine until a native app ships** — which is why ASO is
gated to #3.)

**#1 — Vernacular micro/mid creators (the cold-start spark) · HIGH.** The demo is *inherently
filmable* — watch someone freeze, then talk to the AI. The barrier is fear of judgement; trusted
vernacular creators dissolve it. **Barter / affiliate / rev-share = ₹0 cash**; a hit gives
effective CPI ₹3–15. *Avoid mega-creators.* **Week-1 experiment:** seed 30–50 nano/micro Hindi
creators in the English-learning + jobs niche; brief = "film yourself freezing, then talking to
this AI, then drop the link." Double down on the format that produces hits.

**#2 — WhatsApp shareability + referral (the amplifier) · HIGH.** The §2 loop. Near-zero cost;
access-based rewards (Pro days) gated on **first-session completion** to keep fraud <2%. Sits *on
top of* creator traffic, not a cold-start engine on its own.

**#3 — Google Play ASO + intent search (durable compounder, GATED) · HIGH.** "Spoken English" is
one of India's highest-volume evergreen queries; free + 4.5★ + tiny size compounds rank at ₹0
marginal cost — exactly how **Hello English hit #1 within months.** *Locked until a native app +
accounts ship.* Then: localized Play listings per language, screenshots of a live talking tutor +
mother-tongue feedback; push 4.5★ via an in-app prompt *after a good session*.

**Supporting (₹0 cash):** founder posts **daily Hindi "Speak English without fear" Shorts** → the
PWA. Literally the Josh Talks origin story; the owned-content moat that seeds the first 100k.

**What to NOT do (the discipline that keeps you alive):**
- **Never fund growth with paid ads** (the Byju's grave). Paid = a *measurement tool* only:
  ₹500–1,000/day on exact-intent "spoken English" to benchmark cost-per-*activated*-user.
- **No iOS** (2.5–2.8× costlier, sliver of the user). Android-first / lightweight-first.
- **No celebrity/brand spend.**
- **No telco/OEM/Skill-India bundles or field agents pre-traction** (BD + capital you don't have;
  bundled users retain poorly). Phase 3+.

## 4. Activation & retention (the real killer)

Education-app retention is brutal: D1 ~14–26%, **D30 ~2–7%**, churn 73–96% in 30–90 days. **Fix
D7 BEFORE scaling any channel**, or every creator/rupee just fills a leaky bucket.

- **The aha (<90s, no signup):** first 5-min unscripted talk → freeze → AI fixes it *in mother
  tongue* → retry → don't freeze. Delay accounts as long as possible (local-first is an asset);
  gate signup only at sync / Pro / referral reward.
- **Activation = 3 completed sessions in the first 7 days.** If **D7 < 12–15%, STOP spending** and
  fix onboarding/habit.
- **Streaks done right (sticky but HUMANE):** 5-min daily goal, visible streak, a **free weekly
  streak-freeze + streak-repair**, warm vernacular reminders, **no public shaming.** Punitive
  streaks backfire — when one finally breaks, users quit all at once.
- **Small leagues (20–30 pace-matched learners)** so top-5 stays reachable (+25% completion). Never
  pit a 5-min/day learner against power users.
- **The shareable win = the loop's fuel:** watermarked certificate / level-up card / before→after
  clip at every milestone. Frame **everything** around jobs/status ("Crack your interview," "Speak
  at work without freezing"), plus family angles ("Challenge your sibling" — Groww's "refer your
  parents" drove 40% of signups).
- **Targets:** D1 35–45%, D7 ~20%, D30 10–15%.

## 5. Monetization that compounds with growth

**The biggest threat isn't pricing — it's AI-voice COGS.** Gemini Live realtime ≈ **₹3.2/min** →
a 5-min DAILY free habit = **~₹480/month of raw model cost per active free user**, vs a price floor
of ₹100–150/mo. **Uncapped viral growth on unlimited realtime would bankrupt the company.** Margin
engineering is the whole game — **ship it WITH the live feature, not after.**

**Re-architect the free tier to be cheap, not free-but-expensive:**
- **Free default = ASYNC pipeline:** record → cheap STT → Flash-Lite **text feedback in mother
  tongue** → TTS reply (~an order of magnitude cheaper than realtime Live).
- Free users get a **small daily allowance of live realtime (3–5 min)**; earn more via **rewarded
  video** (each ad offsets the ~₹3.2/min it funds).
- **COGS controls:** per-session minute caps, **idle-timeout to stop billing dead air**, **context
  caching** (~up to 90% input-token cut for the fixed persona/lesson scaffold). Reserve native-audio
  Live for paying/high-intent users. → free-user COGS drops from ~₹480/mo to single-digit ₹.

**Pricing (anchor on annual):**

| Tier | Price | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | ₹0 | Async practice + 3–5 live min/day + rewarded top-ups |
| **Pro (hero = annual)** | **₹999–1,499/yr** ("₹3/day, less than a chai") | Generous live minutes, deep MTI feedback, full course, mock interviews, certificate |
| Pro monthly (fallback) | ₹149–249/mo | same |
| **Micro-packs** | ₹19/30min, ₹49/90min; ₹49–99 mock-interview / level-test / cert | for the ~97% who refuse subscriptions; couples revenue to AI-minute COGS |

**Lead with ANNUAL PREPAID via one-time UPI. Do NOT make UPI AutoPay the primary rail** (~74%
business-decline, ~20M mandate revocations/month). Price ceiling ≈ Duolingo's ₹100/mo; above
₹299/mo you lose mass adoption. **Model on 1–3% conversion, NOT 8%.**

**B2B2C — the only revenue that scales WITH growth** (institutional CAC ₹10–50/student vs ₹150–600
D2C; LTV/CAC >5:1):
1. **Colleges / placement cells first** — ₹100–300/student/yr × 2,000 = ₹2–6 lakh/deal; **200
   colleges ≈ ₹8 Cr ARR** at a fraction of D2C CAC.
2. **Employers / BPOs** — ₹500–2,000/seat/yr; pitch on attrition + ramp-time, offer
   pay-per-certified-hire.
3. **Govt skilling (PMKVY 4.0 / NSDC / Bhashini)** — **funded distribution** to the exact user.
   *Ring-fence it: funded acquisition + credibility, NEVER cash-flow runway* (slow, audit-heavy).

**The model that works (Physics Wallah's):** near-zero-CAC organic growth → small high-ARPU D2C
core → majority of revenue from B2B2C where someone else pays.

## 6. The 0 → 1k → 100k → 1M roadmap

| Stage | Trigger to advance | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **0 → 1k** | D7 ≥ 12–15% on one Hindi cohort | Prove the aha + retention. Keep the PWA. Ship result-card + clip + WhatsApp share. Founder Hindi Shorts. Onboard first users by hand. Scale nothing. |
| **1k → 100k** | Loop K ≥ 0.4 AND ≥2 replicable creator "hits" | Light the spark: 30–50 Hindi creators (barter); turn on the WhatsApp loop; ship async-cheap free tier + COGS caps; pilot 3–5 B2B2C deals. |
| **100k → 1M** | Native app + Play listing + accounts shipped | Unlock ASO; push 4.5★; expand to Telugu/Tamil; layer annual Pro; scale colleges to 50–100. Paid only to *test* creative. |
| **1M → millions** | LTV/CAC >3:1, D30 >10%, B2B2C ARR proven | Pour fuel on what retains: more languages, 100–200 college deals, employer line, govt empanelment; only now consider telco/OEM bundles. |

### First 90 days (lean solo founder)
- **Weeks 1–3 — instrument & build the loop (no growth yet):** local-first analytics (activation,
  D1/D7/D30, invites/user, K/cohort); ship the two-button pre-filled-Hindi WhatsApp share + result
  card + 30-sec before→after clip; onboarding hits the aha in <90s; gentle streaks (freeze +
  repair) + a 20–30 league; lock the release gate (runs on a 3GB-RAM 4G phone; voice degrades to
  push-to-talk).
- **Weeks 4–6 — re-architect economics:** async (STT→Flash-Lite→TTS) free pipeline + 3–5 live-min/day
  cap + idle-timeout + per-session caps + context caching; rewarded-video-for-minutes. Model
  COGS/free-session before promoting anything.
- **Weeks 5–10 — spark + amplifier:** seed 30–50 nano/micro Hindi creators (barter/affiliate, brief
  = freeze→talk→link); founder daily Hindi Shorts; turn on the two-sided "practise-with-me" invite
  (Pro days, gated on first session); ₹500–1,000/day UAC test *purely to benchmark*.
- **Weeks 8–12 — open B2B2C + decide native build:** close 3–5 pilots (free AI "speaking homework"
  to one IELTS centre, one Tier-2 placement cell, one BPO); if D7 ≥ 12–15% AND K ≥ 0.4, start the
  **native Android app + accounts** (the gate to ASO). If not, keep fixing activation.

## 7. Funnel metrics, targets & top risks

Vanity downloads LIE (Hello English's 50M / Stimuler's 4M masked tiny paid bases). Instrument from
day one:

| Metric | Target | Stop-and-fix |
|---|---|---|
| Install → activation (3 sessions / 7 days) | ≥ 25–30% | < 20% |
| **D1 / D7 / D30** | 35–45% / ~20% / 10–15% | **D7 < 12–15% → freeze all spend** |
| K-factor (per cohort) | 0.5–0.7 | < 0.3 → fix share UX/timing |
| Free→paid conversion | **1–3%** (model on this) | — |
| Per-user AI-minute COGS (free) | single-digit ₹/mo | rising → tighten caps/async |
| Cost-per-first-paid-user / LTV·CAC | LTV/CAC > 3:1, payback <12mo | < 3:1 → shift weight to B2B2C |

**Top 3 risks → mitigations**
1. **AI-voice COGS bankrupts you on virality** (~₹480/mo per daily free user). → Async-cheap free
   tier by default, metered/earned live minutes, idle-timeout, caps, context caching — *shipped with
   the live feature.* P0, existential.
2. **India doesn't pay (~1.1% like Stimuler).** → Don't chase millions of *paying* D2C users.
   Annual-prepaid + micro-packs + **B2B2C** (someone else pays) solves acquisition + monetization at
   once. Underwrite the budget on 1–3%.
3. **Leaky-bucket retention — scaling before fixing D7.** → Hard gate: **no channel scales until
   D7 ≥ 12–15%.** Nail the 5-min habit, humane streaks, mother-tongue feedback first.

---

**The path in one sentence:** nail the 5-min daily-habit + mother-tongue aha → light it with
vernacular Tier-2/3 creators (barter) → amplify with a WhatsApp status/clip/"practise-with-me" loop
→ unlock free organic ASO once the native app ships → monetize *later* via a cheap annual Pro +
B2B2C, while keeping AI-voice COGS cheap-by-default — **and never, ever fund growth with paid
acquisition.**
